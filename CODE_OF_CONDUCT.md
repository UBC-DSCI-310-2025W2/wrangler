# Code of Conduct

### DSCI 310 Group 5 Project Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering a kind and respectful environment for learning and collaborative work. All participants in activities related to the DSCI 310 Group 5 Project are required to confirm to the Code of Conduct, and are expected to demonstrate respect and kindness in all spaces, including in communications and work. 

We are dedicated towards supporting a productive, inclusive and welcoming environment for all people, regardless of background and identity. Any discriminatory or disrespectful behaviour, including behaviour that intimidates or causes discomfort to others, will not be tolerated. Everyone who participates in work related to the DSCI 310 Group 5 Project is required to abide by this Code of Conduct both within in-person, and online spaces such as in the lecture room, Slack, Piazza, and Github. 

#### Expected Behaviour
Behaviour expected of participants include demonstrating a willingness to learn and participate in respectful dialogue, openness to constructive criticism and differing perspectives, usage of professional, kind, and inclusive language, and behaving respectfully towards others, in all DSCI 310 Group 5 Project spaces. 

#### Unacceptable Behaviour
Unacceptable behaviour includes any discriminatory comments, violent language or threats, sustained disruption in discussions, or work, and insults or put downs. 

#### Consequences of Unacceptable Behaviour
Violating this code of conduct will result in a report being made to the DSCI 310 teaching team, upon which the teaching team may take appropriate action. 

#### Reporting Guidelines
In the event of a violation of the Code of Conduct, please contact the DSCI 310 instructor or teaching team.
